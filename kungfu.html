The Kung Fu School

I was commissioned by the Kung Fu School NZ to create a fun and engaging flyer that would connect with kids and tell parents about the benefits of their children learning Kung Fu. I used a friendly flat design style to create characters enjoying themselves learning Kung Fu along with a carefree blob style that used vibrant colours to appeal to kids. I divided the benefits of learning into 3 bite sized points for parents to easily understand what these classes offer.
