Schmutz

Schmutz is my personal illustration pesona/style. This is a style that consists of squiggly lines and quirky little characters and plants that always seem to have a habit of filling up the page. Schmutz is a yiddish word for dirt. Although this definition may sound like an unpleasant word to describe ones work, I like to redefine Schmutz as a description for 'stuff and things'.
