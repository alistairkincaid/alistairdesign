Bird Series

This project stems from my fear of birds. Cassowaries in particular freak me out! However, illustrating them in my quirky cartoon style makes them feel more friendly and allows me to face my fear. I can reassure myself 'caso-don't-wary'. Our flamingo friend on the right is a single pringle and ready to mingle (although he may look a bit apprehensive).
