GridFree

GridFree is a solar panel company that offers kiwis D.I.Y. off grid solar kits. I've created newspaper ads that showcase GridFree's products and used photography and language to appeal to those living on Great Barrier Island. I have produced 5 instruction manuals for each of the different products. These manuals have a focus on functionality and health & safety while maintaining a readable and clean design aesthetic.

I assist GridFree as their film maker and editor for YouTube marketing ads, 'how to videos' and interviews with customers.
