Experience a New Place

Experience a New Place is a one minute narrative designed to show the importance of travel. In 2018 I had an experience of a life time travelling to the Middle East. On this trip I learnt so much about other cultures and felt I had a deeper understanding of the world outside New Zealand. This is what inspired me to create a motion piece on travel. When we travel we are exposed to different cultures, locations, history, food and music. This allows us to gain a deeper understanding of other cultures. As a result we are more empathetic towards one another.

In this story we follow an individual caught up in his own bubble. He goes on a journey, travelling through different locations and by the end of it he has a better appreciation for the world around him. I was assigned the art movement Bauhaus for inspiration. I combined a geometric design with a casual illsutration style. My aim here was to create a carefree travel mood with the illustration and use vibrant colour and the geometric Bauhaus shapes to bring the scene to life.

This project was recognised by Media Design School and in association with Auckland Live was played in a reel of student work on the Digital Stage in Aotea Square early 2019.
