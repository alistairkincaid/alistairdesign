Tyres

I've worked part time as the Marketing Production Assistant at Exclusive Tyre Distributors. This was a position that involved a variety of different tyre related design and marketing tasks. I was tasked with creating flyers for the Micky Thompson and Cooper Tires brands when there was a release of new tyres or deals. I would create marketing campaigns which would include digital and physical collateral.

Other roles were to update and maintain many websites for Cooper Tire dealers. I designed home page banners, web imagery and created engaging SEO titles and content.

I created two billboards that were put up in 2019 in East Tamaki, Auckland. One was showcasing the tread depth of Cooper Tires. The other was a part of a Cooper Tires Rugby campaign. In this project I photographed the subject, ex All Black Bruce Gemmell.
