Make-o-saurus

I was commissioned by Project Make to create a persona for a dinosaur called Make-o-saurus Rex (Make-o). I had a lot of creative freedom in this project and I used this as a chance to incorporate my squiggly line work which I refer to as Schmutz. This project was an excellent learning curve for me, it pushed me to illustrate new content such as dinosaurs while maintaining my own style.

In my Schmutz style I tend to draw abstract 2D plants and I gathered inspiration from prehistoric plants to develop a world for Make-o. I used this project to learn new skills technically such as character rigging, animating liquids, different transitions and experimenting with textures.

From the get go Make-o was always going to be a T-Rex. His personality is a creative critter that quietly hides behind the vegetation as he gets busy 'making'. We only catch a glimpse of Make-o in amongst the Schmutzy vegetation while the excitement of his creations unfold in front of us. Creativity is in all of us, to be inclusive I didn't want to limit Make-o to any particular skill. We follow him creating science experiments, cooking, exploring, and coming up with new ideas.

We can look at Make-o as a source of inspiration for our own work, he'll always be there in the background giving a helping claw.
